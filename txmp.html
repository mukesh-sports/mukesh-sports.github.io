Fantasy Akhada is a fantasy sports platform and this platform provides you to play Fantasy Cricket, Fantasy Football, Fantasy Kabaddi, Fantasy Basketball and Fantasy Hockey by using your sport knowledge and skill. You can pick your own team made up of real players for Cricket, Football, Kabaddi, Basketball & Hockey. You need to create your team within a maximum allowed budget of 100 credits. Your team will earn points based on your chosen players' performance in the real – life matches. Yes, you get to feel the same adrenaline rush when your chosen player scores a century or a hat-trick. Sounds fun right?

This indeed is the moment to show the world what a fantastic Fantasy Sports Manager you are! Come Play with us

All you need to do is follow these 5 simple steps to get going:

Step 1: Choose A Match

Choose any of the upcoming matches from any of the current or upcoming cricket series

Step 2: Create Your Team

You have 100 credits available to create your Fantasy Akhada team by using your sports knowledge and an opportunity to showcase your skills

Step A: Choosing the match

First choose any upcoming match from the list of ongoing and upcoming cricket series and then click on the ‘Create Team’ button

Step B: - Building the Team

Create your Fantasy Akhada team by picking 11 players as per the below combinations (Combo 1, Combo 2, Combo 3 and so on...) within a budget of 100 credits.

Do check for player info by clicking on the player image and you also have the option to sort players as per their teams, credits or points.

Watch out for the number of players added in your team, credits left and of course the deadline to the game

Step C: Choosing your Captain and Vice-Captain

After you have chosen your Fantasy Akhada XI, you must choose your Captain and Vice-Captain for your team.

Remember, Captain gets 2X the points scored by him in the actual game AND

Vice-Captain - Gets 1.5x points scored by him in the actual game

Step D: Creating multiple teams

You have the option of creating up to 50 teams per match and choose to join a contest with any of the teams that you have created!

Click on 'CREATE TEAM' for creating next team

Step E: Managing your team – Use the ‘Edit Team’ Button

Make multiple changes to your Fantasy Akhada Team as your wish until the deadline of the match by using the “Edit Team” button on the page.

This feature also lets you make multiple changes to your Captain or Vice-Captain before the deadline of the match.

Do keep an eye out on which of your players are playing the match and keep your session updated at all times.

Step 3: Join a Contest

Join any Fantasy Akhada free or cash contest to win ultimate bragging rights or cash to show-off your improvement in the Free/Skill contests on Fantasy Akhada! You can also compete with your friends through a private league created by you or your friends

Step 4: Follow the Match

Watch the real match and track your fantasy scorecard (updated on an on-going basis) & celebrate your chosen players’ mind-blowing performances

Step 5: Withdraw your Winnings

Instantly withdraw your winnings from your Fantasy Akhada account (One Time Verification required)
